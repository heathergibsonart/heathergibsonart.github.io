Meet Heather Gibson

I remember always being drawn to art since I was a very young child. I believe art is a very important part of life, whether that be expressed through the visual arts, music, literature, or theater. I am interested in contrasting ideas, sounds, and imagery expressed through various mediums. I find humans and our connections to one another, to nature, and to the physical world around us as the main inspiration for my work. I love art that is meaningful but also art for art's sake. A 2016 graduate of West Virginia State University with a BA in Art
